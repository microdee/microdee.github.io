Description

LBRNTH is an interactive installation presented at the exhibition "Hová lesznek a kitörölt fájlok?" and LPM 2012. It was projected on the ground. People could stand on the playing area and they could balance the levels like they were standing on a raft on the water. The goal is to take the ball from the start (yellow spot) to the end (green hole) avoiding falling down from the level or falling in the holes.
